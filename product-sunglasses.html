Sustainable Design Excellence

EcoVision has revolutionized eyewear manufacturing by transforming ocean-bound plastic waste into premium fashion accessories. Each pair prevents approximately 20 plastic bottles from entering our oceans, while the biodegradable acetate temples ensure end-of-life sustainability.

The Italian-designed frames combine timeless aesthetics with modern functionality. Available in six colorways, from classic tortoiseshell to contemporary transparent blue, each design complements various face shapes while making a bold sustainability statement.

Advanced UV Protection Technology

Protection is paramount, and EcoVision delivers with their proprietary TripleShield lens technology:

  • 100% UV Protection: Blocks all UVA/UVB rays up to 400nm
  • Polarization: Eliminates 99.9% of reflected glare
  • Blue Light Filter: Reduces digital eye strain by 40%
  • Anti-Reflective Coating: Minimizes lens reflections
  • Hydrophobic Layer: Repels water and oils

Environmental Impact

Every purchase contributes to ocean cleanup initiatives. EcoVision partners with coastal communities to collect ocean-bound plastic, creating jobs while protecting marine ecosystems. To date, the company has prevented over 2 million plastic bottles from entering the ocean.

The packaging is equally thoughtful – made from recycled cardboard with soy-based inks, and includes a microfiber cleaning cloth crafted from recycled polyester. Even the protective case uses cork harvested sustainably from Mediterranean forests.
