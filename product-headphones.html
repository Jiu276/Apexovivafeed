Exceptional Sound Quality

The SoundPro Studio Headphones feature custom-engineered 40mm drivers with rare-earth neodymium magnets, delivering a frequency response of 4Hz to 40kHz. This extended range captures subtle details often lost in compressed audio, from the deepest sub-bass to crystalline highs.

The soundstage is remarkably wide for closed-back headphones, creating an almost speaker-like presentation. Instrument separation is exemplary – in complex orchestral pieces, you can pinpoint individual instruments within the mix. The tuning strikes a perfect balance: neutral enough for critical listening, yet engaging enough for casual enjoyment.

Industry-Leading Active Noise Cancellation

The hybrid ANC system employs eight microphones to achieve up to 42dB of noise reduction. In our testing, this completely eliminated airplane cabin noise, reduced traffic sounds to a whisper, and made open offices bearable.

  • Transparency Mode: Natural pass-through for awareness

Smart Features and Connectivity

Bluetooth 5.3 ensures rock-solid connectivity with minimal latency. The multipoint connection allows simultaneous pairing with two devices – seamlessly switching between laptop and phone. LDAC support delivers near-lossless audio quality for compatible devices.

The companion app offers extensive customization: 5-band EQ, soundstage adjustment, and ANC intensity control. Firmware updates have consistently improved performance since launch, adding features like spatial audio support and auto-EQ based on ear shape analysis.

Outstanding Battery Life

The 30-hour battery life with ANC enabled is class-leading. In real-world testing, we achieved 32 hours at moderate volume. Without ANC, battery life extends to an impressive 45 hours. The fast-charging capability is genuinely useful – 10 minutes provides 5 hours of playback.

The headphones intelligently manage power, automatically entering sleep mode when removed and instantly waking when worn. The wear detection is flawless, pausing playback immediately when removed.

Crystal Clear Call Quality

The six-microphone array with beamforming technology delivers exceptional call quality. AI-powered wind reduction and voice isolation ensure your voice comes through clearly, even in noisy environments. Multiple colleagues commented that calls sounded better than our desk phone.
